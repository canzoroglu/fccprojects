Mustafa Kemal Atatürk

The man who saved Turkish People's life and give us sovereignity of our state
Atatürk, which means father of Turks, is the founder of Republic of Turkey

Here's a time line of Atatürk's life:

  • 1881 - Born in Selanik, Ottoman Empire
  • 1888 - Ali Rıza, Mustafa’s father, dies.
  • 1893 - Mustafa enters military preparatory school in Selanik.
  • 1889 - Mustafa Kemal enters infantry class of War College in Istanbul.
  • 1902 - Commissioned Second Lieutenant, and enters Staff College.
  • 1905 - Passes out as Staff Captain and is posted for Fifth Army in Syria; revives a secret opposition group in Damascus.
  • 1919 - Kemal lands in Samsun.
  • 1920 - Kemal opens the Grand National Assembly (BMM) in Ankara.
  • 1921 - The battle of Sakarya begins with Turkish troops led by Mustafa Kemal. Kemal receives the rank of Marshal and the title Gazi (veteran, victorious warrior).
  • 1923 - Proclamation of the Republic of Turkey.
  • 1934 - The Grand National Assembly of Turkey passes a law granting him the surname "ATATÜRK".
